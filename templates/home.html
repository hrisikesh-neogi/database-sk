OUR SHOHOR!

Founded in 2020 by the THREE IDIOTS, our establishment has been serving up rich bengali culture sourced from passionate cultural folks in various regions of West bengal and Bangladesh. We are dedicated to travelling the world of culture, finding the best ideas and creativities, and bringing back to you here in our shohor. We guarantee that you will fall in LOVE with our SHOHOR the moment you walk inside. Join us for your PASSION to be in a daily routine, an nourishment of your personality, or simply just to enjoy some good time being in a Family. Shohure kotha open to all, and you can join us for any kind of cultural activities.
